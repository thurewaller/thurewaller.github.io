A Christmas Gift Filled With Photos

Combining two of my greatest passions in life, my girlfriend and photography, this is what one might call a real passion project. My girlfriend had long been complaining that she never got to see my photographs. Therefore I decided to make her a Christmas gift filled with photos. The book features photos from our trips and adventures.

 

I enjoy making things and therefore I decided to bind the book myself. The result is a 168 pages photo book with a simple layout that lets the images take centre stage. Scroll down to see the process and final result.

Selection and Layout

With quite a few adventures together in the last years there were many photos to choose from. After a first rough selection the images were printed and put on a wall. Next step was to decide the order. With the order set I did the final layout in InDesign before sending it to print.

Learning by Doing

Having never bound a book before I had to do some research. I borrowed books about the subject and searched the internet for guides. Lacking some of the proper tools I had to improvise with the things I had at home. The sewing went fine and after just one failed attempt with the cover it all came together fine. The result was a much appreciated Christmas gift.
