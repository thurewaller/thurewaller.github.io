Gritty Covers for Crime Novels

Anders Karlsson started his career as a transportation journalist in the mid 1990s. He has through the years build a deep understanding of the transportation industry and was awarded “Transportation Journalist of the Year” in 2006. Since 2008 he is the Editor-in-Chief of the leading magazine for transportation and logistics in Sweden.

 

Besides the work as Editor-in-Chief Anders writes crime novels taking place in the transportation industry. The first book called “Hållhake” was released in 2014. This book was followed by “Onda Tankar” in 2015 and “Kuppen” in 2016.

 

Anders asked me to design the covers for “Onda Tankar” and “Kuppen”. The covers should go together with the cover of the first book. We therefore decided to keep a similar colour scheme and a rough style of illustrations. To establish a connection to the plot, elements from the transportation industry were incorporated.
