THE SHORT

I’m Thure Waller, a designer and human being trying to become an even better designer and human being.

THE LONG

With a graphic designer as father and a grandmother obsessed with collecting antiques my interest in design started early. At the age of eleven I found Photoshop and my passion for design grew stronger.

 

Apart from spending hours and hours in Photoshop, ice-hockey was a large part of my upbringing. Early Sunday mornings on the outdoor rink, long travels for away games, wins and defeats shaped me into the great team player I am today.

 

Growing up on the Swedish countryside made me resourceful. I learned that no problem is too big. With an open-mind, the right dose of imagination and a “never giving up”-attitude anything can be accomplished.

 

My curiosity and hunger to learn led me to the Industrial Design Engineering-program at Chalmers University of Technology. Here I learned to apply human-centered design methodology to solve complex problems.

 

Now I’m looking for new challenges and would like to join a creative and diverse team where we can help each other become the best we can be. Do you know the right team? Send an email to hello@thurewaller.com or call +46 730 32 12 71.
