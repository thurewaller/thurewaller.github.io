Energising a Petrol Station Brand

Sjömacken Hunnebostrand is a petrol station for boats located in one of the most beautiful places along the Swedish West Coast. To match the beautiful surroundings and the high ambitions of the new owner it was time to freshen up the brand. Sjömacken wanted their brand to be professional yet modern with a strong connection to the maritime. Scroll down to see what I came up with!

IDEATION

From Sketches to Concepts

Modern maritime became the theme for the concept development. A large number of sketches were done to explore different ideas for the logotype. Out of all sketches four ideas were developed further and presented to the client.

FINAL DESIGN

Building the Identity

The client really liked the first of the four concepts and we decided to move on and create the brand around it. The symbol has a minimalistic design referencing a droplet, a ship and upward pointing arrows. The blue colours further incorporate the maritime vibe and add energy to the brand.
