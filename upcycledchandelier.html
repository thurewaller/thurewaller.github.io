One Man's Trash is Another Man's Treasure

Upcycling is taking something used and giving it new functionality and added value. This project is an exploration in upcycling and how material choices impact the experience of products. Chandeliers are commonly associated with high quality and luxury. By making a chandelier out of cheap disposable plastic spoons and trash our preconceptions are challenged.

 

The chandelier is made from things I had laying around; 283 disposable plastic spoons, an old bicycle wheel and some fishing line. When lit the chandelier casts interesting shadows on the walls of the room. Scroll down to see the result.
